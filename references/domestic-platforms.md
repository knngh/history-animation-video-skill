# Domestic Platforms

Use this reference when recommending Chinese publishing platforms for historical AI animation videos. Verify official requirements when exact limits, monetization, AI labels, or platform policy details matter.

## Recommended Stack

Primary:

- 抖音: fastest short-video testing, strongest recommendation velocity, best for aggressive hooks and high-frequency iteration.
- 快手: strong private-domain and trust-based distribution, good for clear storytelling and familiar historical figures.
- 微信视频号: best for WeChat social sharing, public account linkage, and mature/parent audiences.
- 小红书: best for cover design, visual aesthetics, notes, women-focused history, and saveable knowledge cards.
- B站: best for deeper context, series, bullet comments, long-tail search, and community identity.

Secondary:

- 西瓜视频: useful for medium-length explainers and ByteDance ecosystem repackaging.
- 知乎: useful for source-grounded answers, text/video hybrids, and authority building.
- 微博: useful for hot-topic discussion, short clips, and quick debate.
- 百家号: useful for Baidu search exposure and text/video republishing.

## Platform Fit

抖音:

- Publish 35-75 seconds first.
- First frame and first line must create tension immediately.
- Best formats: reversal, last choice, "history remembers this wrong", one object changed history.
- Packaging: large subtitles, strong cover text, fast visual changes.

快手:

- Publish 45-90 seconds.
- Make the story easier to follow and less abstract.
- Best formats: loyal minister, family dilemma, war decision, familiar textbook figure.
- Packaging: human feeling, clear conflict, less polished but more sincere narration.

微信视频号:

- Publish 45-120 seconds.
- Aim for shareability inside WeChat groups and public account ecosystems.
- Best formats: moral dilemma, family-safe history, patriotic but factual framing, historical wisdom.
- Packaging: title should be clear; avoid excessive shock language.

小红书:

- Publish 30-75 seconds, plus carousel notes when possible.
- Best formats: women in history, character aesthetics, historical clothing, "facts vs drama", quote cards.
- Packaging: cover text must be clean, notebook-like, and saveable.

B站:

- Publish 90-240 seconds for richer stories, or vertical shorts for testing.
- Best formats: source-based analysis, animated explainers, series by dynasty/character.
- Packaging: title should include knowledge gain; description can include sources and caveats.

西瓜视频:

- Publish 1-5 minute explainers after short-video validation.
- Best formats: fuller historical context, maps, timelines, documentary voice.
- Packaging: less clickbait, more clear episode promise.

知乎:

- Publish answer-first posts with embedded video.
- Best formats: "How should we evaluate X?", "Was X really wrong?", "What is misunderstood about X?"
- Packaging: cite sources, separate facts from interpretation, avoid pure promotion.

微博:

- Publish 30-90 second clips with concise debate copy.
- Best formats: hot historical anniversaries, controversy, quote cards, quick thread.
- Packaging: one claim plus one question; use hashtags carefully.

百家号:

- Publish text + video versions for Baidu search.
- Best formats: evergreen biographies, event explainers, "one minute understand" search topics.
- Packaging: search-friendly titles, clear keywords, less slang.

## Cross-Post Rules

- 抖音 and 快手 can share the same core footage, but change cover text and first sentence.
- 视频号 needs a more shareable and less aggressive title.
- 小红书 needs a cleaner cover and note-style caption.
- B站 needs more context or a series label.
- 知乎 and 百家号 need source-aware text versions.
- 微博 needs a debate line, not a full essay.

## Domestic Launch Order

Start with:

1. 抖音 for fast hook testing.
2. 小红书 for visual/cover testing.
3. B站 for community and series testing.
4. 视频号 for shareability testing.
5. 快手 for broader trust-based reach.

Then repurpose into 西瓜、知乎、微博、百家号 once the best topics are identified.

## Metrics

- 抖音/快手: 3-second retention, completion rate, comments per 1,000 views, follows.
- 视频号: shares, friend interactions, profile visits.
- 小红书: saves, cover click-through, comments, follows.
- B站: completion, bullet comments, coins, favorites, follows.
- 知乎/百家号: search traffic, reads, saves, credible comments.
- 微博: reposts, comments, topic spread.
