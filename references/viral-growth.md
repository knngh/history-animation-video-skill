# Viral Growth

Use this reference when optimizing historical AI animation videos for fast spread. The goal is high retention and sharing without fake history, impersonation, or misleading AI content.

## Viral Thesis

A historical short spreads when it gives viewers one of these feelings quickly:

- "I had this person wrong."
- "This choice is more dangerous than I thought."
- "I would argue about this in the comments."
- "This looks like a movie but teaches me something."
- "I want part two."

Do not optimize for empty shock. The best format is "surprise first, proof second, debate last."

## Hook Types

Use one hook per video:

- Wrong-memory hook: "你记住的【人物】，可能是错的。"
- Price hook: "他赢了，但代价比失败更大。"
- Last-night hook: "这是【人物】人生最安静，也最危险的一夜。"
- Forbidden-choice hook: "如果你是他，这一步你敢不敢走？"
- Betrayal hook: "真正改变结局的，不是敌人，是身边人。"
- Object hook: "这封信/这张地图/这道命令，改变了他的一生。"
- Reversal hook: "历史说他赢了，可他从这里开始输了。"

Reject weak hooks:

- "今天讲讲某某的一生。"
- "你知道某某吗？"
- "三分钟带你了解某某。"
- "历史上有一个很厉害的人。"

## Retention Design

For 35-75 seconds:

- 0-1s: visual shock or contradiction on screen.
- 1-3s: one sentence open loop.
- 3-8s: tell viewers why they should care.
- 8-20s: add pressure and one concrete object.
- 20-40s: reveal the choice.
- 40-60s: show the cost.
- Last 3s: debate prompt or part-two loop.

Add a pattern interrupt every 2-4 seconds:

- face close-up
- hand/object close-up
- map movement
- quote card
- cut to crowd reaction
- sudden silence
- title stamp
- battlefield/court wide shot

## Title Formulas

- 【人物】最危险的一步，不是你以为的那一步
- 他赢了战争，却输给了胜利之后
- 如果你是【人物】，这道命令你敢下吗？
- 历史记住了他的功劳，却漏掉了他的代价
- 【人物】真正的敌人，可能不在战场
- 一封信，让【人物】再也回不了头
- 这一夜之后，【人物】不再是原来的他

## Cover Rules

- 3-7 Chinese characters or 2-5 English words.
- One face plus one object is usually stronger than a busy scene.
- Use a question or danger word, not a summary.
- Keep the cover readable at phone thumbnail size.

Cover text examples:

- 他敢吗
- 没有退路
- 赢后的代价
- 最后一夜
- 真相反转
- The Cost
- No Way Back
- Last Choice

## Comment Triggers

End with a question that has two defensible answers:

- "如果你是他，你会保天下，还是保身边人？"
- "这是果断，还是赌徒式冒险？"
- "他是改变历史的人，还是被历史推着走的人？"
- "你觉得他最大的错误，是太狠，还是不够狠？"

Avoid begging:

- "喜欢点个关注"
- "你怎么看"
- "评论区告诉我"

## A/B Test Pack

For each video, generate:

- 5 titles: curiosity, conflict, emotion, reversal, search-friendly.
- 3 covers: face/object, quote card, decisive moment.
- 3 first lines: question, contradiction, dangerous scene.
- 2 endings: debate prompt and part-two prompt.

Publish the same core video with platform-specific packaging, not identical copy everywhere.

## Viral Quality Gate

Before publishing, reject the video if:

- The first sentence could apply to any historical figure.
- The viewer can predict the ending by second 5.
- There is no irreversible decision.
- The script has more background than conflict.
- The ending does not create a reason to comment, save, or watch part two.
- The historical claim is spicy but unsupported.
